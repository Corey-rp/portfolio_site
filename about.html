About Me

The person
behind the code

I'm Corey Richards-Persad, a Computer Science student at Georgia State University with a passion for building software that solves real problems. I'm not just learning concepts in the classroom — I'm shipping actual products.

My most recent project is the MSA Secure Pickup Package, a full three-app system I built from scratch for the Morehouse College STEM Achievers Summer Program. It handles everything from parent registration and QR code generation to staff scanning and SMS alerts — all deployed and live.

I bring a professional background in customer service, team leadership, and operations, which shapes how I think about user experience and communication. I know what it's like to work with real users under pressure, and that mindset carries into every app I build.

My goal is to land a role where I can grow as a developer, contribute to meaningful products, and keep building things that make a difference.
